NIR (NDVI) Sensor/Camera


This Sensor captures near-infrared and blue light video/photos in the same image, but in different color channels. This imagery can be post-processed to determine the level of photosynthesis. Typical IR is night vision, however NIR can be captured during daytime flight. This system was developed for the Ag industry, but there are many uses. The data collected gives real time information of the crop health in order to increase yield
